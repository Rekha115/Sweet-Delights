Our Story

Founded in 1985 by master baker Jacques LeBlanc, Sweet Delights began as a small neighborhood bakery with a passion for traditional French techniques and the finest ingredients.

Today, we continue Jacques' legacy with the same commitment to quality, now serving the entire community with our award-winning pastries, breads, and cakes.

All our products are made from scratch daily using organic flour, European butter, and seasonal local ingredients whenever possible.
